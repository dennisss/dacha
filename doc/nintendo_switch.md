

Battery
- 5-pin
- 3.7V LiPo battery
- Middle pin is 'TH' (thermal)
    - 10K thermistor
    - ~9.1K to ground during room temperature
    - ~3K when hot
- Internal
    - Uses HC77AY (thermal cutoff)
